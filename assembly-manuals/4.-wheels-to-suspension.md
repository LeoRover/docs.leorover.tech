# 5. Wheels and tires to suspension mounting

## Intro

### Assembly time

30 minutes + 50 minutes cable soldering

### Tools needed

* [ ] no. 5 hex-head key
* [ ] cable isolation tape/tubes
* [ ] cable cutters
* [ ] soldering iron / any other way to connect two cables
* [ ] \(optional\) cyanoacrylate glue
