# Battery Box assembly

![1. At first mount button and socket with 3 cables.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_160411.jpg)

![2. Second step is to mount 3D printed cover.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_161151.jpg)

![3. Mount square nuts into 3D printed part.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_161219.jpg)

![4. Mount 3D printed part with torx bolts but leave them loose.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_161312.jpg)

![5. For next step prepare bottom part of battery box and 4 M4x16 bolts.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_161438.jpg)

![6. Assemble everything together - make sure that button is on the centre of hole in metal part.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_161648.jpg)

![7. Tighten torx bolts. ](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_161708.jpg)

![8. Solder battery, button and socket according to this scheme. Black cable from battery and socket should be soldered together to the one pin from button](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_160522.jpg)

![9. After soldering put distance sponge at the bottom of the box.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_162844.jpg)

![10. Insert battery ](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_162905.jpg)

![11. Close the cover. Make sure that everything is inside and you didn&apos;t squeeze any cable.](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_163223.jpg)

![12. And that&apos;s it!](../.gitbook/assets/20190731_163232.jpg)
