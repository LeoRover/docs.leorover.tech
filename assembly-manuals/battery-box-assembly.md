# Task 6: Battery quarter \(left\) assembly

## Intro

### Assembly time

10 minutes

### Tools needed

* [ ] T5x40 torx screwdriver \(provided\)
* [ ] no. 3 hex-head key \(metric; provided\)
* [ ] wide cross-headed screwdriver
* [ ] soldering iron
* [ ] heat shrink tube \(for battery cables isolation\)
